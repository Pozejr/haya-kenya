Support Networks

Explore the support networks and communities available to assist individuals affected by HIV/AIDS.

Community Support Groups

Join local community support groups where you can:

  • Share experiences and stories
  • Receive emotional support
  • Access information on HIV/AIDS
  • Participate in advocacy efforts

Online Forums and Resources

Connect with others through online platforms and resources:

  • Online forums and chat rooms
  • Webinars and virtual support groups
  • Informational websites and resources
  • Social media groups and pages

Counseling and Mental Health Services

Access professional counseling and mental health services:

  • Individual counseling sessions
  • Group therapy sessions
  • Mental health resources and referrals
  • 24/7 helplines and crisis support

Peer Support Networks

Engage with peer support networks that offer:

  • Mentorship and guidance
  • Peer-to-peer counseling
  • Activities and events
  • Skill-building workshops

For more information about support networks or if you need assistance, please feel free to contact us.
