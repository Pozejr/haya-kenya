Our Programs

We offer a variety of programs aimed at promoting awareness, prevention, and support for HIV among the youth in Kenya. Our programs are designed to educate, empower, and provide resources for young people affected by HIV.

Awareness Campaigns

Our awareness campaigns aim to educate the youth about HIV prevention, transmission, and treatment options.

Support Groups

We offer support groups for young people living with HIV, providing a safe space to share experiences and receive emotional support.

Testing Services

We provide free and confidential HIV testing services to help youth know their status and take appropriate steps.
