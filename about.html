About Us

Welcome to HIV Youth Alliance Kenya. We are committed to supporting and empowering youth affected by HIV through education, resources, and community programs.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide education, support, and resources to youth affected by HIV in Kenya. We strive to create a supportive community where young people can learn, grow, and thrive.

Our Vision

Our vision is a world where every young person affected by HIV has the opportunity to live a healthy, fulfilling life free from stigma and discrimination.

Our Values

  • Empowerment: We believe in empowering youth to take control of their health and future.
  • Education: We are committed to providing accurate, up-to-date information about HIV.
  • Community: We foster a sense of community and belonging among our members.
  • Support: We offer emotional and practical support to those affected by HIV.
