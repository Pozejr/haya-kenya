About Us

Our History

Founded in 20XX by [Founder's Name], HIV Youth Alliance Kenya emerged from the vision of empowering and supporting people living with HIV (PLHIV) in Kenya. [Founder's Name], the driving force behind our organization, is also living with HIV, bringing firsthand understanding and empathy to our mission.

Our Mission

At HIV Youth Alliance Kenya, our mission is to empower and support people living with HIV (PLHIV) by providing comprehensive care, education, and advocacy. We strive to enhance the quality of life for PLHIV through accessible healthcare, community engagement, and promoting awareness.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a society where every person living with HIV has access to equitable healthcare, lives free from stigma and discrimination, and leads a fulfilling life with dignity.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We approach our work with empathy and understanding, ensuring all individuals receive compassionate care.
  • Empowerment: We empower PLHIV to make informed decisions about their health and well-being.
  • Integrity: We uphold the highest ethical standards in all aspects of our work, fostering trust and accountability.
  • Collaboration: We collaborate with partners, communities, and stakeholders to achieve sustainable impact.
  • Excellence: We are committed to delivering excellence in healthcare services, education, and advocacy.

Our Impact

Since our inception, we have reached thousands of individuals through our programs, providing vital support and resources to enhance their health and well-being. Our initiatives in testing, treatment, and advocacy continue to make a positive impact in the fight against HIV/AIDS.
