HIV Testing

Getting tested for HIV is crucial for early diagnosis and access to treatment. HIV Youth Alliance Kenya promotes awareness and accessibility to HIV testing services.

Why Get Tested?

Testing for HIV is important for:

  • Early detection and treatment
  • Prevention of transmission to others
  • Access to care and support services

Types of HIV Tests

There are different types of HIV tests available:

  • Antibody tests
  • Antigen/antibody tests (4th generation)
  • Rapid tests
  • Home testing kits

Where to Get Tested?

You can get tested at:

  • Local health clinics
  • HIV testing centers
  • Community health events
  • Mobile testing units

Confidentiality and Support

HIV testing is confidential, and support services are available to assist you throughout the testing process.

If you have any questions about HIV testing or need support, please feel free to contact us.
