Community Resources

Explore community resources available to support individuals affected by HIV/AIDS in Kenya.

Local Support Groups

Connect with local support groups offering:

  • Peer support and group sessions
  • Information sharing and community events
  • Advocacy and awareness campaigns
  • Access to resources and referrals

Online Platforms and Forums

Engage with online communities and platforms for:

  • Virtual support groups and discussions
  • Access to educational resources
  • Networking with individuals and organizations
  • Sharing experiences and information

Healthcare and Treatment Centers

Access local healthcare facilities offering:

  • HIV testing and counseling services
  • Antiretroviral therapy (ART) and medical care
  • Mental health and supportive care services
  • Referrals to specialized HIV/AIDS care

Legal and Social Services

Get assistance with legal and social needs:

  • Legal advice and advocacy services
  • Social welfare support and assistance programs
  • Rights protection and discrimination prevention
  • Financial and housing support initiatives

For more information about community resources or if you need specific assistance, please feel free to contact us.
