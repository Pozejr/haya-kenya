Counseling Services

Explore counseling services offered by HIV Youth Alliance Kenya for individuals affected by HIV/AIDS.

Types of Counseling

We provide various types of counseling to meet your needs:

  • Individual Counseling: One-on-one sessions with a trained counselor to address personal issues and concerns related to HIV.
  • Group Counseling: Participate in group sessions to connect with others, share experiences, and receive support.
  • Family Counseling: Guidance and support for families affected by HIV to improve communication and understanding.
  • Mental Health Counseling: Services to address mental health challenges associated with living with HIV/AIDS.

Benefits of Counseling

Benefits of counseling include:

  • Emotional support and understanding
  • Coping strategies and stress management
  • Improving overall mental and emotional well-being
  • Enhancing quality of life and resilience

Accessing Counseling Services

Our counseling services are accessible through:

  • Local HIV clinics and treatment centers
  • Community health programs and organizations
  • Referrals from healthcare providers
  • Virtual counseling options and telehealth services

For more information about counseling services or to schedule a session, please feel free to contact us.
